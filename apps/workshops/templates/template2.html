Your Visit

The AIM Facility in Pasadena AIM is located on the eight floor of Caltech Hall on the Caltech campus in Pasadena. The newly renovated space has a lecture area and seminar rooms designed for AIM style workshops and collaborative research. AIM visitors can connect to the Caltech network, and AIM has a printer for common use. Visitors should bring their own laptops. During workshops a variety of drinks and snacks are available.
